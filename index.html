About Me

Data Explorer.
Problem Solver.
Builder.

I'm Shwetha R, a BCA student at Acharya Bangalore Business School, Bengaluru. With a CGPA of 9.41 and a Department Topper award, I've consistently pushed beyond the academic syllabus to build real, working solutions.

"I don't just visualise data — I make it speak. Every dashboard tells a story, every model makes a prediction, every system solves a problem."

My journey began with a curiosity about why things fail — why crops wither, why businesses lose revenue, why libraries still run on paper. That curiosity became my engineering compass. I've since built an IoT plant monitoring system, a sales analytics dashboard in Power BI, and a full web-based library management system.

I'm passionate about the intersection of AI, data analytics, and real-world impact. I'm actively building my expertise in Machine Learning, AWS cloud, and advanced data analytics while pursuing certifications alongside my degree.
